Discover Kampung Mersilau

Experience the breathtaking beauty and cool mountain air of Kundasang's hidden gem, right at the foothills of Mount Kinabalu.

Explore Attractions

Must-Visit Attractions

Explore the stunning sights in and around Kampung Mersilau.

Desa Dairy Farm

Known as the 'Little New Zealand' of Sabah. Enjoy fresh dairy products, feed the calves, and soak in the magnificent scenery.

Mersilau Highlands

Witness unparalleled views of Mount Kinabalu, wander through vibrant vegetable farms, and breathe in the crisp, cool air.

Sosodikon Hill

A short hike rewards you with a stunning 360-degree panoramic view of the surrounding valleys and the majestic Mount Kinabalu.
