Kampung Mesilau is inhabited by the Dusun community, one of the largest and most deeply rooted ethnic groups in Sabah. Their way of life is based on a strong sense of family, ancestral traditions, and a robust agricultural culture. Although modern influences and Christianity have transformed parts of their lifestyle, cultural values such as the use of the Dusun language, communal work (gotong-royong), and respect for the natural environment are still actively practiced. This makes the Dusun ethnic group in Kampung Mesilau a prime example of a community that adapts to changing times without losing its original identity.
