Basic Preparation

As for the transport rent a car or 4WD from Kota Kinabalu or Kundasang because at Kampung Mesilau, public transport is very limited. Also make sure your vehicle is in good condition. Becasue Mesilau roads are steep, narrow, and winding. As for the expenses during your vacation there please bring enough cash. Because many places in Mesilau are cash-only, nearest ATM are in Kundasang or Ranau.

At the Kampung Mesilau the temperatures can drop below 15¬∞C, especially early morning and night. So you must bring with you warm clothes such as hoodies, windbreakers, and thick socks. As for the health and safety essentials, don't forget to bring first aid kit, personal medications, insect repellent (mosquitoes and midges are common), suncreeen and lip balm for dry mountain air.
