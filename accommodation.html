HOMESTAY: Ahleeyado Mesilau

Ahleeyado Mesilau Homestay is a charming village homestay located in the cool highlands of Kampung Mesilau, Kundasang. Nestled along C140, Jalan Golf Course Mesilau, this homestay welcomes visitors looking for a peaceful escape surrounded by fresh mountain air, lush pine forests, and breathtaking views of Mount Kinabalu.

Staying at Ahleeyado Mesilau gives guests the chance to experience true Sabahan village life while enjoying comfortable rooms and warm local hospitality. Here, families, couples, or small groups can wake up to misty mornings, the sound of chirping birds, and stunning sunrise views right from their doorstep.

The homestay is ideally located near the famous Mount Kinabalu Golf Club, one of Southeast Asia’s highest golf courses, where guests can tee off with majestic mountain scenery as their backdrop. Other nearby attractions include vibrant vegetable farms, flower gardens, and scenic trails perfect for nature lovers and photographers alike.

Kampung Mesilau itself is a hidden gem for travelers seeking adventure, relaxation, or a break from the city heat. With its cool climate, friendly villagers, and beautiful natural surroundings, every visit promises unforgettable memories high above the clouds. Whether you plan to climb Mount Kinabalu, explore Kundasang’s famous markets, or simply relax and breathe in the fresh highland breeze, Ahleeyado Mesilau Homestay is ready to make your stay special and memorable.

CHALET: Mesilau Cliffs Homestay – Chalet 1

Mesilau Cliffs Homestay – Chalet 1 is a spacious and charming mountain chalet nestled along Jalan Cinta Mata Mesilou, Kundasang, Sabah. Situated approximately 1,600 metres above sea level, this chalet provides guests with a refreshing highland retreat surrounded by panoramic views of Mount Kinabalu and the beautiful pine-forested valleys that characterise Mesilau.

This large chalet comfortably accommodates up to 10 guests across four well-appointed bedrooms, making it an ideal choice for families, groups of friends, or those planning a special getaway in the cool highlands. The chalet features two clean bathrooms, a cozy living room perfect for spending time together, and a fully equipped kitchen where guests can prepare meals with ease.

Guests can unwind on the spacious terrace while enjoying a relaxing barbecue with family and friends, or spend quiet evenings in the lounge area, breathing in the crisp mountain air. Modern conveniences such as free Wi-Fi, complimentary parking, and access to a shared garden and lounge ensure a comfortable stay without sacrificing the charm of a rustic highland escape.

Mesilau Cliffs Homestay – Chalet 1 is strategically located just a short drive from well-known local attractions such as the Mount Kinabalu Golf Club, vegetable farms, flower gardens, and Kundasang’s fresh produce markets. For nature lovers, the surrounding area offers scenic trails, breathtaking sunrise views, and peaceful spots for photography or quiet reflection.

Whether you are planning a family holiday, a group retreat, or a cozy mountain escape, Mesilau Cliffs Homestay – Chalet 1 invites you to experience the beauty of Mesilau’s highlands. Here, guests can enjoy the cool climate, warm local hospitality, and the unforgettable feeling of being high above the clouds, surrounded by nature’s best.

CAMPSITE: The Mountain Camp at Mesilau by PrimaStay

The Mountain Camp at Mesilau, Kundasang by PrimaStay is a well-regarded campsite and glamping site situated along Jalan Golf Course Mesilau, nestled approximately 1,600 metres above sea level. This highland retreat offers visitors a refreshing escape amidst cool mountain air, misty mornings, and spectacular panoramic views of Mount Kinabalu and its surrounding pine forests.

The camp provides both traditional tent camping grounds and comfortable glamping units, catering to travellers who wish to experience the outdoors while still enjoying modern amenities. Each unit is designed with convenience in mind, offering private bathrooms, hot shower facilities, Wi‑Fi access, barbecue pits, and fully equipped kitchens. A private creek located within walking distance provides guests with a tranquil spot to unwind, while clear night skies make the campsite ideal for stargazing and bonfire gatherings.

Guests consistently commend The Mountain Camp for its excellent hospitality, cleanliness, and well-maintained grounds. Many appreciate the safe and peaceful environment, as well as its proximity to nature trails and hidden waterfalls. Its strategic location also places it within easy reach of the Mount Kinabalu Golf Club, nearby vegetable farms, and Kundasang’s popular local attractions.

Whether for families, couples, or small groups of adventure enthusiasts, The Mountain Camp at Mesilau offers a memorable glamping experience that combines the rustic charm of camping with the comforts of modern accommodation — all amidst the natural beauty of Kundasang’s highlands.

LODGE: Little Hut Mesilau

Little Hut Mesilau is a cosy lodge-style cabin retreat located in the cool highlands of Kampung Mesilau, Kundasang. Nestled among lush pine forests and overlooking magnificent views of Mount Kinabalu, this lodge offers guests a peaceful escape surrounded by fresh mountain air and serene village scenery.

Staying at Little Hut Mesilau allows visitors to experience the tranquillity of Kundasang while enjoying warm wooden cabins, comfortable bedding, and private balconies that open out to misty mountain views. It is an ideal choice for couples, families, or small groups wanting to relax and reconnect with nature.

This lodge is conveniently located near local attractions such as vegetable farms, flower gardens, and the renowned Mount Kinabalu Golf Club — one of Southeast Asia’s highest golf courses. Guests can also enjoy barbecue facilities, picnic spots, and quiet evenings under the starry highland sky.

Kampung Mesilau is a hidden highland gem for travellers seeking cool weather, friendly locals, and beautiful natural surroundings. Whether exploring local markets, enjoying sunrise views, or simply unwinding in a rustic cabin, Little Hut Mesilau promises a comfortable and memorable stay.
